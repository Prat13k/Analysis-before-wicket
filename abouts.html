Who We Are

Analysis Before Wicket is a data-driven cricket analytics platform. We provide deep insights, performance analysis, and head-to-head statistics. Our goal is to combine cricket knowledge with modern data science to give fans and analysts a smarter way to understand the game.

Our Mission

To make cricket analytics accessible, insightful, and actionable. From team performance to player stats and predictive analysis, we aim to deliver information that enhances the way cricket is followed and understood.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted hub for cricket analytics, bridging the gap between data, strategy, and the passion of the sport.
